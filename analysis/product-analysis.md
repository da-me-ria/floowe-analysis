## Product summary

Floowe is an AI-powered content creation and publishing platform that mimics the work of a professional copywriter to help businesses create, optimize, and automatically distribute articles and social media posts. It focuses on improving SEO and online visibility by generating high-quality, keyword-optimized content and publishing it across websites and major social media channels from one place.

## Target users

- **Small and medium businesses**: Companies that want to increase their online visibility and SEO without hiring full-time copywriters or agencies.
- **Marketing teams and social media managers**: Teams responsible for content marketing, social media presence, and SEO who need to scale content production and automate distribution.
- **Solo professionals and creators**: Consultants, freelancers, and creators who want to present their knowledge and offers attractively while saving time on writing and posting.
- **Agencies and content providers**: Agencies managing content and social media for multiple clients who benefit from centralized, automated publishing and SEO-optimized content.

## Core features

- **AI-assisted article generation**: Generates article ideas and full articles by analyzing popular content and key phrases relevant to the business and audience.
- **Professional copywriting simulation**: Mimics the process of an experienced copywriter to produce unique, SEO-optimized texts quickly.
- **Simple WYSIWYG editing**: Allows easy editing of generated articles with an interface similar to a Word document.
- **Graphics management in articles**: Offers selection of graphics (AI-related mention plus realistic, licensed photos or user-uploaded images) to accompany content.
- **Automatic social media post generation**: Creates social media posts (e.g., snippets, summaries) from each article.
- **Multi-channel automatic publishing**: Automatically publishes posts to Facebook, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and to the user’s own website via a plugin.
- **SEO-focused keyword optimization**: Analyzes and injects popular key phrases and keywords similar to those used in paid SEO to improve rankings.
- **Multi-language support**: Supports content generation in many languages, especially European languages, to reach international audiences.
- **Content personalization controls**: Lets users set style, tone, topics, and keywords to match brand identity and audience.
- **Account-based personalization and analytics**: Uses information about the business and target audience to personalize content and offers basic result analysis (e.g., SEO impact and social performance).
- **Security and privacy controls**: Provides encrypted data handling and compliance with regulations such as GDPR/RODO.
- **Tiered subscription plans**: Offers free and paid plans with different limits on articles and features.

## Main user flows

- **Onboarding and account setup**
  - User registers for a Floowe account.
  - User provides information about their business, target audience, and goals.
  - User connects their website (via plugin) and links social media accounts (Facebook, LinkedIn, X).
- **Content ideation and generation**
  - User selects or inputs topics/keywords or lets Floowe analyze popular content in their niche.
  - Platform generates a list of article ideas.
  - User selects preferred ideas and triggers article generation.
  - Floowe produces draft articles with embedded SEO keywords.
- **Content editing and enhancement**
  - User opens the generated article in a Word-like editor.
  - User edits text, adjusts structure, and personalizes style and tone.
  - User selects or uploads graphics for the article.
- **Social content creation and distribution**
  - Based on the article, Floowe automatically generates social media posts.
  - User reviews and optionally edits the generated posts.
  - User schedules or confirms automatic publishing to connected social channels and website.
- **SEO and performance follow-up**
  - Over time, Floowe tracks impact on SEO (e.g., improved positions in Google).
  - User reviews results and iterates by generating new articles and posts.

## UX/UI patterns

- **Simple, marketing-focused landing page**: Clear hero section highlighting AI-assisted content creation and publishing with a strong “Test for free” call-to-action.
- **Step-based explanation of workflow**: Three-step explanation of how to generate and publish content, lowering perceived complexity.
- **Plan comparison section**: Clear, vertically listed pricing cards (Free, Basic, Standard, Premium) with feature bullets and visible monthly price.
- **Testimonials and social proof**: Customer quotes and “Companies that trust us” section to build credibility.
- **Benefit-first messaging**: Copy emphasizes outcomes such as SEO improvement, visibility, speed, and ease rather than just technical details.
- **Multilingual elements**: Presence of both English and Polish content fragments implies language-localized experiences.

## Business model

- **SaaS subscription model**: Floowe is offered as a subscription-based web application with recurring monthly payments.
- **Tiered pricing plans**:
  - Free plan with a low limit (e.g., 3 articles per month) and core automatic posting features.
  - Paid plans (Basic, Standard, Premium) priced approximately at 150 €, 200 €, and 250 € per month respectively.
- **Feature and usage-based differentiation**: Higher plans include increased article quotas and potentially additional features like more advanced SEO analysis and more publications per month.
- **Free trial / freemium funnel**: “Test for free” and a free tier encourage low-friction onboarding that can later convert to paid plans.

## Potential integrations

- **Content Management Systems (CMS)**
  - WordPress and other CMS platforms via plugins for automatic article publishing.
  - Headless CMS platforms (e.g., Contentful, Strapi) to push generated content into existing content pipelines.
- **Social media platforms**
  - Direct integrations with Facebook, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter) for post scheduling and publishing.
  - Possible extension to other social platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Community, Pinterest) for broader reach.
- **Marketing automation and CRM tools**
  - Email marketing platforms (e.g., Mailchimp, Sendinblue, HubSpot) to reuse generated content in newsletters and campaigns.
  - CRM systems (e.g., HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce) to align content with lead nurturing and track campaign effectiveness.
  - Marketing automation suites to trigger content generation or publishing based on lifecycle events.
- **Analytics and SEO tools**
  - Google Analytics and Google Search Console to measure traffic and ranking changes from Floowe-generated content.
  - SEO tools (e.g., Ahrefs, Semrush, Moz) to feed keyword research data into Floowe for better topic and keyword selection.
- **Collaboration and productivity tools**
  - Integration with tools like Slack, Microsoft Teams, or project management platforms for content reviews, approvals, and notifications.
